circularstats.distributions.coeffs_wrapcauchy¶

static distributions.coeffs_wrapcauchy(a, mu, k_comp=10, neg=False)¶

coefficients for series expansion of wrapped cauchy distribution

These are also the circular moments of the wrapped cauchy distribution based on Fourier series expansion

Parameters :

x : array_like

points at which density is evaluated

mean : float (or array_like ?)

mean of underlying normal distribution

a : float (or array_like ?)

parameter of underlying cauchy distribution

k_comp : int

number of terms (including zero term) to include in the series representation

neg : bool

If neg is true, then the coefficients are also for negative moments, that is coefficients for range(-k_comp + 1, k_comp) Warning: not used, not tested, might not be necessary
Returns :

coeff_cos, coeff_sin : ndarray

The cosine and sine parts of the coefficients for the series expansion.
