circularstats.api.circ_vtest¶

static api.circ_vtest(alpha, mu0, w=1, d=0)¶

V test for uniformity of circular data with a specified mean alternative.

H0: the population is uniformly distributed around the circle
HA: the population is not distributed uniformly around the circle but
has a mean of ``mu0``.

Note: Not rejecting H0 may mean that the population is uniformly distributed around the circle OR that it has a mode but that this mode is not centered at dir.

The V test has more power than the Rayleigh test and is preferred if there is reason to believe in a specific mean direction.

Parameters :

alpha : array_like

data, sample of angles in radians

mu0 : float

mean direction under the alternative

w : float or ndarray

case weight, number of observations in case of binned angle data

d : float

spacing of bin centers for binned data. If supplied, then correction factor is used to correct for bias in estimation of r, in radians.
Returns :

pval : float

p-value of V test

v : float

value of the V statistic

References

Biostatistical Analysis, J. H. Zar

Philipp Berens, 2009, Circular Statistics Toolbox for Matlab
